About Us

At Breezy Rides, we make every journey stress-free, comfortable and reliable. Whether you’re a local or here to explore Sikkim’s breathtaking beauty, we’re here to get you where you need to go – quickly and hassle-free. We offer a modern fleet of clean, well maintained vehicles driven by experienced professionals who know the roads like the back of their hand.

At Breezy Cabs we are more than just a ride. We’re committed to sustainability and eco-tourism, doing our part to preserve Sikkim’s natural beauty while delivering top-notch service. With us, your journey isn’t just from one place to another – it’s about experiencing the joy of travel. Sit back, relax and let us take you on a ride you’ll love. With Breezy Cabs, every journey feels like a breeze!

History

When & why Breezy Cabs was founded?

With a passion for travel and hospitality, Sunil Gurung founded Breezy Cabs in 2025 with the vision of making Sikkim’s beauty accessible to everyone with ease. With experience of guiding and driving for more than a decade he knows how hospitality has changed ever since and how it has to be redefined with the needs of time.

He has created a fleet of professional guides and pilots who have become brothers and understands his vision of redefining cab rental services. 80% of his clients are referred and many among them have actually encouraged him to make his service professional; hence the birth of Breezy Cabs happened.
